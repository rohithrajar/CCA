VISION & MISSION

Vision

Expand opportunities for our students to acquire a high degree of understanding of Commerce and Computer Knowledge and thereby become world-class businessmen and worthy citizens of society. Empower excellent education in the profession of Commerce with Computer Applications and renovate the students into eligible Commerce professionals, facilitating the achievement of social and economic goals.

Mission

Our mission is to:
  • Impart in-depth knowledge in core Commerce courses.
  • Provide hands-on experience in the application of Commerce knowledge in a computerized business environment.
  • Inculcate ethical, moral, and spiritual values to meet the challenges of the commercial scenario.
  • Secure good academic results in the University Examination.
  • Impart knowledge through a student-centric, innovative teaching and learning process.
  • Train and develop students with employable skills required for the Commerce and IT sectors.
