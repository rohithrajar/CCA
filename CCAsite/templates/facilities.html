Our Department Highlights

Computer Lab

A dedicated computer lab with 72 computers and free high-speed internet. Students can use it for projects, research, and practical learning.

Department Library

A small but well-stocked library with textbooks, reference books, and journals for both students and teachers to study and prepare for exams.

ICT Smart Classroom

A digital classroom with modern teaching tools. It helps teachers use presentations, videos, and interactive methods to make learning easier.

Experienced Research Guide

The department has an eminent research guide who gives expert advice and support to students and staff interested in research work.

Question Bank

A collection of past University question papers from April 2013 to April 2025. It helps UG and PG students understand exam patterns and practice well.

First Aid Kit

A fully equipped first aid kit is always available to give quick help in case of any minor injury or emergency.
