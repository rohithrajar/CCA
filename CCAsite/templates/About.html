About Us

Department of Commerce with Computer Application

The P.G. Department of Commerce with Computer Application was established as the Department of Bachelor of Commerce with Computer Applications (B.Com-CA) in 2001. The sanctioned student strength is approximately 50. An additional section was introduced during the academic year 2009-2010 to fulfill the needs of rural students. In 2012, the Post Graduate course M.Com (CA) was started. Currently, the department is functioning with 13 eminent professors. Nine students have achieved University Rank until April 2019.

Department Highlights

  • Provides in-depth knowledge about subjects through quality education.
  • Offers student skill programs like PC Software & MS-Office, C, C++, DBMS, SQL, Tally, python, and Software Web Development.
  • Conducts Guest Lectures, Seminars, Workshops, and Industrial Visits.
  • Encourages participation in extracurricular activities and communication skill programs at Inter-collegiate, State, and National levels.
  • Prepares students for Post Graduation courses and professional courses like CA and ICWA.
  • Provides dual employment opportunities in Commerce and IT.
  • Grooms students to become outstanding professionals in line with corporate expectations.
  • A dedicated computer lab with 72 computers and free high-speed internet. Students can use it for projects, and practical learning.
